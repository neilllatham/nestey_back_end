API Use Cases:
Benefits:
"My benefits": shows what medical, dental and vision plans
"what have i paid": shows how much ytd an employee and employer has paid and notes paid period as bi weekly
"medical details": show medical plan name and description, how much the employee pays and the employeer
"when is enrollment": shows the window as 11/1/2025 through 11/30/2025
“When can I add or remove a dependent?”	Life events (marriage, birth, loss of coverage).
“How much do I pay vs. what the company pays?”	Cost breakdown per pay period (employer vs. employee contribution).
“When does my coverage start?”	Waiting periods or effective dates tied to hire date.
“When is open enrollment?”	Annual window for plan changes and key deadlines.
